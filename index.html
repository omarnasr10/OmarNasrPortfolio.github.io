Project Overview:

This project involved comprehensive data analysis of a Bike Sales dataset, focusing on leveraging SQL and Power BI to derive actionable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.

Objectives:

  • Data Cleaning and Transformation: Ensure data quality and prepare it for analysis.
  • Sales Performance Analysis: Identify top-selling products, regions, and sales trends.
  • Customer Segmentation: Understand customer demographics and purchasing patterns.
  • Inventory Management: Optimize stock levels based on sales forecasts.
  • Interactive Dashboard Creation: Develop a dynamic Power BI dashboard for real-time monitoring and reporting.

Steps and Technologies:

The project followed a structured approach, utilizing the following technologies:

  • SQL Server: For data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) processes, including complex queries, joins, and aggregations.
  • Power BI: For creating interactive dashboards and reports, featuring various visualizations like bar charts, line graphs, pie charts, and tables to present insights effectively.
  • Data Modeling: Designed a robust star schema data model in Power BI to facilitate efficient data analysis.
  • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Developed custom measures and calculated columns in Power BI to enhance analytical capabilities.
  • Data Cleaning: Utilized SQL and Power Query in Power BI for extensive data cleaning, handling missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Performed initial data exploration to understand patterns and anomalies.
SQL Power BI DAX ETL Data Modeling Data Visualization

Key Outcomes:

The project delivered a comprehensive Power BI dashboard that provides stakeholders with a clear view of key sales metrics, enabling them to make data-driven decisions. Insights gained include identification of peak sales seasons, popular bike models, and effective marketing strategies. The solution empowers the business to optimize inventory, target customer segments more effectively, and improve overall sales performance.

This project was entirely developed using Power BI and Power Query, focusing on analyzing employee performance, job satisfaction, and salary trends. The goal was to provide a data-driven story that guides stakeholders through key insights.

Storytelling Approach:

The analysis follows a structured storytelling approach, beginning with an overview and progressively diving deeper into specific aspects of employee data.

  1. Summary:
    • Provides a high-level view of the dataset.
    • Highlights key metrics and trends to give an instant understanding of the overall employee landscape.
  2. Employee Overview:
    • Analyzes employee demographics and general attributes.
    • Includes details on departments, job roles, tenure, and experience levels.
    • Helps identify patterns and distributions in employee data.
  3. Salary Analysis:
    • Explores salary distributions across different job roles and experience levels.
    • Examines salary trends over time to identify growth patterns.
    • Helps in understanding the impact of experience and position on compensation.
  4. Performance & Rating Analysis:
    • Focuses on employee satisfaction and performance evaluations.
    • Analyzes the relationship between job satisfaction, manager ratings, and training opportunities.
